About GURU

Teaching since 2018

The teaching excellence of R. Priyadharshini is marked by her profound mastery of Bharatanatyam and her innovative approach to training. Her ability to blend traditional techniques with creative choreography, particularly in her signature peacock dance, sets her apart as a visionary educator. With a deep commitment to fostering individual expression, she instills discipline, confidence, and emotional depth in her students. Her teaching is not only rooted in technical precision but also in nurturing the artistic soul of each student, creating a learning environment where trust, loyalty, and creativity flourish. R. Priyadharshini’s exceptional mentorship has garnered recognition, transforming Bharadham360 into a hub for artistic excellence.

Dance Training

We Offer

01

Grade Exam

Grade exams at Bharadham360 provide students with a structured opportunity to assess their proficiency in Bharatanatyam. These exams, aligned with academic standards, evaluate students on various aspects of the dance form, including technique, expression, rhythm, and understanding of the repertoire. Conducted under the guidance of R. Priyadharshini, these exams help track a student's progress and provide them with clear goals for improvement. Beyond mere evaluation, they also instill a sense of discipline, commitment, and confidence in students, preparing them for both performances and future endeavors in the field of Bharatanatyam.

02

Diploma Course

Bharadham360 offers diploma courses in Bharatanatyam in affiliation with Bharathidasan University, providing students with an opportunity to gain formal certification in this classical art form. These courses are designed to enhance both the theoretical and practical understanding of Bharatanatyam, ensuring a structured learning experience under expert guidance. By combining the expertise of Bharadhan360 with the academic recognition of Bharathidasan University, these programs serve as a significant milestone for students aspiring to pursue Bharatanatyam professionally or academically.

03

Salangai Pooja

Salangai Pooja at Bharadham360 is a sacred and significant ceremony that marks the beginning of a student’s journey into Bharatanatyam. Performed before the commencement of formal dance training, this ritual involves the worship of Lord Nataraja and the blessing of the salangai (traditional ankle bells), symbolizing the student’s commitment and dedication to the art form. Under the guidance of R. Priyadharshini, the ceremony fosters a deep spiritual connection to Bharatanatyam, setting the foundation for discipline, devotion, and respect for the dance. It is an important cultural and emotional event, signifying the student's initiation into the world of classical dance with reverence and gratitude.

04

Arengetram

At Bharadham360, Arangetram is a significant milestone in a student's Bharatanatyam journey, marking their formal debut as a solo performer. The academy provides thorough training and guidance to students preparing for this grand event, ensuring they are well-equipped to showcase their mastery of the art form. The Arangetram at Bharadham360 is a culmination of years of rigorous practice, focusing on perfecting the Margam, which includes pure dance, expressive performances, and devotional items.

05

Cultural Events

Cultural events at Bharadham360 provide students with valuable opportunities to showcase their talent and gain performance experience in front of diverse audiences. These events serve as platforms for students to demonstrate their skills, creativity, and growth as artists. Under the guidance of R. Priyadharshini, the students actively participate in various local and regional cultural events, where they gain exposure and develop their stage presence, confidence, and competitive spirit. These events not only allow students to celebrate their achievements but also help foster a deeper connection to the cultural traditions of Bharatanatyam

06

Government Events

Bharadhan360 organizes various cultural events throughout the year to enrich the learning experience of its students. These events include dance performances, workshops, and festivals that provide students with opportunities to showcase their talents, engage with the community, and connect with other artists. Notable events include student recitals, solo and group performances, and themed dance showcases. The academy also hosts workshops with guest artists and collaborative events with other cultural organizations. These events not only foster artistic growth but also help students gain practical performance experience, develop stage presence, and deepen their understanding of Bharatanatyam's cultural significance.
